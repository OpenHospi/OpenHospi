## How does the chat work?

When a lister accepts your response to a listing, a chat conversation is automatically opened. From that moment you can exchange messages.

### End-to-end encryption

All messages on OpenHospi are **end-to-end encrypted**. This means:

- Messages are encrypted on your device
- Only the recipient can decrypt them
- OpenHospi cannot read your messages
- Even if our servers are hacked, your messages remain safe

### Sending messages

Open a conversation and type your message in the input field. Press send and your message is immediately encrypted and delivered.

You can:

- Send text messages
- View conversation status

### Notifications

You'll receive a notification when you get a new message. Make sure you have notifications enabled in your device settings.

<Callout type="warn">
  Never share sensitive information like your BSN, bank details, or passwords in the chat. Although
  your messages are encrypted, it's always wise to be careful.
</Callout>

## Blocking and reporting

Feel unsafe or receiving inappropriate messages? You can block or report a user:

- **Block:** The user can no longer send you messages
- **Report:** Our team reviews the report and takes action if needed

Both options can be found in the conversation menu (three dots in the top right of the conversation).
