## Safe room hunting

Looking for a room can be exciting. Use these tips to protect yourself against common risks.

### Before you respond

- **Check the profile** — See if the lister has a complete profile with photo and description
- **Be critical** — If an offer seems too good to be true, it probably is
- **Ask for details** — Ask questions about the house, neighbourhood, and housemates before making an appointment

### First contact

- **Use the OpenHospi chat** — Communicate via our platform so everything is encrypted
- **Don't share sensitive information** — Never give out your BSN, bank details, or a copy of your ID
- **Never pay upfront** — Don't transfer money before you've seen the room and signed a contract

<Callout type="error">
  OpenHospi never asks for payments through the platform. If someone asks you to transfer money
  before viewing the room, it's likely a scam. Report it immediately.
</Callout>

### During the viewing

- **Bring someone along** — Don't go to a viewing alone, or tell someone where you're going
- **Visit during the day** — Schedule your visit in daylight
- **Trust your instincts** — If something doesn't feel right, leave

### After finding a room

- **Read the contract** — Take time to read the rental contract before signing
- **Take photos** — Document the condition of the room when you move in
- **Keep everything** — Save contracts, payment receipts, and correspondence

## Seen something suspicious?

Report suspicious behaviour directly via the report function in the app, or email **info@openhospi.nl**.
