OpenHospi is and will remain free for students. This is possible thanks to the support of sponsors and partners who believe in accessible student housing.

<Callout type="info">
  OpenHospi has no premium model or paywalls. Sponsorship makes it possible to maintain and improve
  the platform at no cost to users.
</Callout>

## Why sponsors?

Developing and maintaining a platform costs money: servers, domain name, security, and development time. Instead of passing those costs on to students, we chose a sponsorship model.

## Become a sponsor

Are you an organisation that wants to support students? Get in touch via **info@openhospi.nl** or visit our [Open Collective page](https://opencollective.com/openhospi).
