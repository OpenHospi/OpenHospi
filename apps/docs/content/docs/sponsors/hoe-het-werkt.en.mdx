## Sponsorship model

OpenHospi operates with a transparent sponsorship model via [Open Collective](https://opencollective.com/openhospi). All income and expenses are public.

### What we use sponsorship funds for

- **Servers & infrastructure:** Hosting, database, storage, and monitoring
- **Domain & certificates:** openhospi.nl and SSL certificates
- **Development:** New features and improvements
- **Security:** Audits and security measures

### What sponsors do not get

- Access to user data
- Influence over platform content
- Advertising space on the platform

<Callout type="info">
  Sponsorship does not grant access to data or influence over the platform. Sponsors support the
  project, nothing more.
</Callout>

## How to become a sponsor

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Get in touch**

    Email info@openhospi.nl or visit our [Open Collective page](https://opencollective.com/openhospi).

  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Choose a contribution**

    You can contribute one-time or monthly. Every amount helps.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Visibility**

    Sponsors are mentioned on our website and in the documentation as thanks for their support.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Individual contributions

You can also support OpenHospi as an individual via [Open Collective](https://opencollective.com/openhospi). Every contribution, big or small, helps us keep the platform free for students.
