A good profile increases your chances of finding a room. Housemates want to know who they'll be living with, so show them who you are!

## Filling in your profile

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Add a profile photo**

    Upload a clear photo of yourself. Profiles with photos get far more responses.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Basic information**

    Fill in your name, age, field of study, and the city where you're looking.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    **About me**

    Write a short introduction. Share something about your hobbies, study habits, and what you're looking for in a living situation.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Set your preferences**

    Indicate what's important to you: budget, location, room type, and your lifestyle (quiet or social, early bird or night owl, etc.).

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for a great profile

<Tabs items={["Seekers", "Listers"]}>
  <Tab value="Seekers">
    - Be honest about your budget and preferences - Mention your field of study and what year you're
    in - Briefly describe your daily routine (helps with matching) - Add a personal touch. It makes
    your profile less anonymous
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Listers">
    - Describe the house and its atmosphere - Mention house rules if there are any - Indicate what
    kind of housemate you're looking for - Be clear about practical matters (shared costs, cleaning
    schedule, etc.)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout type="info">
  Your profile is only visible to other verified students on OpenHospi. Non-logged-in visitors
  cannot see your information.
</Callout>
