## General

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="What is OpenHospi?">
    OpenHospi is a free, open-source platform where students in the Netherlands can find or list a
    room. All users are verified students via InAcademia.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is OpenHospi really free?">
    Yes, completely. There are no hidden costs, premium subscriptions, or paywalls. OpenHospi is
    funded by sponsors and donations.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who is OpenHospi for?">
    For anyone who is a student at a Dutch educational institution connected to InAcademia (most
    universities and colleges).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is OpenHospi available outside the Netherlands?">
    Currently, OpenHospi focuses exclusively on the Dutch student market. We may expand in the
    future.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Account & Login

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="How do I create an account?">
    Click "Log in" and sign in with your student account via InAcademia. Your account is created
    automatically. See [Create an Account](./aan-de-slag/account-aanmaken) for a detailed guide.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My institution isn't listed, what now?">
    Not all institutions support InAcademia. Contact your school's IT department and ask if they
    support InAcademia. You can also email us at info@openhospi.nl.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I log in with a regular email address?">
    No. To ensure all users are verified students, you can only log in via InAcademia with your
    student account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
    Go to your profile settings and click "Delete account". All your data will be permanently
    removed.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Rooms & Listings

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="How do I create a room listing?">
    Go to "List a Room" in the navigation and follow the steps. See [Create a
    Listing](./kamer-aanbieden/listing-aanmaken) for a detailed guide.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How many rooms can I list at once?">
    You can list multiple rooms at the same time, as long as they are actually available.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I temporarily take my listing offline?">
    Yes, you can pause a listing. It won't be shown to seekers, but you can reactivate it later.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Chat & Privacy

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Are my messages really private?">
    Yes. All messages are end-to-end encrypted. Even OpenHospi cannot read them. Only you and your
    conversation partner have access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What do I do if someone behaves inappropriately?">
    You can block and report the user via the conversation menu. Our team reviews every report.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What data does OpenHospi collect?">
    Only what's necessary: your email address (via InAcademia), profile data you fill in yourself,
    and minimal analytics. See [Your Data](./privacy-en-veiligheid/jouw-gegevens) for the full
    overview.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
