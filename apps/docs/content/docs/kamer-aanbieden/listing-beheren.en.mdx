## Managing your listings

On your dashboard you'll find an overview of all your active and paused listings. From there you can manage everything.

### Editing

Click on a listing to adjust the details. You can change the title, price, description, photos, and availability at any time. Changes are visible immediately.

### Pausing

Don't have a room available temporarily, but don't want to delete your listing? Pause it. Paused listings are not visible to seekers, but you can reactivate them later.

### Deleting

Once the room is definitively rented, you can delete the listing. This cannot be undone.

<Callout type="info">
  Don't forget to delete or pause your listing once the room is occupied. This keeps the platform
  up-to-date for everyone.
</Callout>

### Viewing responses

For each listing you'll see an overview of all students who have responded. You can view their profiles and decide who you'd like to chat with.

When you accept a response, a secure chat conversation is automatically opened.
