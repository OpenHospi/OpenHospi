## Listing your room

Creating a listing only takes a few minutes. Follow the steps below.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Click "List a Room"

    You'll find this button in the navigation bar or on your dashboard.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Fill in the basics

    - **Title** — A short, catchy title (e.g., "Spacious room in central Utrecht")
    - **Price** — The monthly rent including/excluding service charges
    - **Type** — Self-contained, shared, studio, etc.
    - **Available from** — When the room is available

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Add photos

    Upload at least 1 photo of the room. More photos mean more responses. Tips:
    - Take photos in daylight
    - Show the whole room, not just a corner
    - Include photos of shared spaces (kitchen, bathroom, garden)

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Write a description

    Describe the room, the house, the neighbourhood, and your housemates. Be honest and thorough — this prevents questions and attracts the right candidates.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Publish your listing

    Review everything and click "Publish". Your listing is immediately visible to all verified students.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  You can edit or temporarily pause your listing at any time. See [Manage Your
  Listing](/docs/kamer-aanbieden/listing-beheren) for more information.
</Callout>
