Welcome to the OpenHospi documentation! Here you'll find everything about finding or listing a room, using the chat, and how we protect your privacy.

<Callout type="info">
  OpenHospi is completely free and open source. You can only log in through your educational
  institution (InAcademia), so every user on the platform is a verified student.
</Callout>
