## Showing interest

Found a room you like? Here's how to get in touch with the lister.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open the listing

    Click on the room that interests you to view the full details.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Click "Respond"

    At the bottom of the listing you'll find the button to respond. This sends a request to the lister.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Wait for a response

    The lister receives a notification and can view your profile. If there's interest, a chat conversation will be opened.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Start the conversation

    Once the lister accepts your request, you can continue chatting via the secure chat. All messages are end-to-end encrypted.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warn">
  Never share personal information like your BSN (citizen service number), bank details, or
  passwords via chat. OpenHospi will never ask for these either.
</Callout>

## What does the lister see?

When you respond to a listing, the lister can see:

- Your profile photo and name
- Your age and field of study
- Your "About me" text
- Your housing preferences

Make sure your profile is complete and up-to-date for the best chance of a response.
