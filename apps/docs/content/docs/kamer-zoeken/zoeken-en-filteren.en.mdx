## Finding rooms

On the discovery page you'll see all available rooms. You can refine results with filters so you only see rooms that match your needs.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open the discovery page

    Go to the "Discover" tab in the navigation. Here you'll see an overview of all available rooms.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Set your filters

    Use the filter bar to refine your search:

    - **City** — Choose the city where you want to live
    - **Price** — Set your minimum and maximum budget
    - **Room type** — Studio, shared room, self-contained, etc.
    - **Available from** — Filter by availability date

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Browse the results

    Scroll through the listings. Each listing shows a photo, price, location, and a short description.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### View the details

    Click on a listing for all the details: photos, room and house description, housemate profiles, and conditions.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  Listings are automatically sorted by relevance. Rooms that match your profile preferences appear
  higher in the results.
</Callout>

## Tips for effective searching

- **Be flexible with your budget** — set a slightly wider range than your ideal price
- **Check regularly** — new rooms are added daily
- **Complete your profile** — listers check your profile before responding
