## What we collect

OpenHospi only collects data that is necessary to make the platform work. We never sell your data and don't use it for advertising.

### When creating your account

- **Email address:** Received via InAcademia, used for your account
- **Student verification:** A confirmation that you're a student (we don't receive your student number)

### When using the platform

- **Profile data:** What you fill in yourself: name, photo, field of study, "about me" text
- **Listings:** Rooms you offer, including photos and descriptions
- **Messages:** End-to-end encrypted, we cannot read the content
- **Usage data:** Minimal analytics to improve the platform (no tracking of individual behaviour)

## Where we store your data

- **Database:** Supabase (EU region), secured with Row-Level Security
- **Files:** Photos are stored in Supabase Storage (EU)
- **Messages:** Encrypted on your device, only the recipient can read them
- **Monitoring:** Sentry (EU, without personally identifiable information)

<Callout type="info">
  All our servers are in the EU. We comply with the GDPR and do not process data outside the
  European Economic Area.
</Callout>

## Managing your data

You always have control over your own data:

- **View:** See all your data in your profile settings
- **Edit:** Update your profile, photos, and preferences whenever you want
- **Delete:** You can permanently delete your account and all associated data via settings

When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed, including profile information, listings, and chat history.

## Contact

Have questions about your privacy or want to submit a request? Email **privacy@openhospi.nl**.
