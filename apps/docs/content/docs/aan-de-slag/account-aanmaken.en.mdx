## How to create an account

OpenHospi uses **InAcademia** to verify that you're a student at a Dutch educational institution. You don't need to create a separate password — you log in with your existing student account.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Go to OpenHospi

    Open [openhospi.nl](https://openhospi.nl) in your browser or download the app.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Click "Log in"

    You'll be redirected to the InAcademia login page. Select your educational institution from the list (e.g., Utrecht University, Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences, etc.).

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Log in with your student account

    Enter your student email and password — the same credentials you use to log in at your institution.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Done!

    After successful verification, you'll be redirected back to OpenHospi. Your account is automatically created and you can get started right away.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warn">
  Is your institution not listed? Your school may not yet support InAcademia. Contact your
  institution or email us at info@openhospi.nl.
</Callout>

## What is InAcademia?

InAcademia is a service by SURF (the ICT organisation for Dutch education) that verifies you're a student without sharing unnecessary personal data. OpenHospi only receives:

- A confirmation that you're a student
- Your email address (to link your account)

Your password is **never** shared with OpenHospi.
